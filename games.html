Apollyon

Apollyon is the first game I worked on. This game is about exploring a cave where you find horrors beyond anything humanity has ever seen before. For this project, I did the sound design and level design. I am really proud of my work.















Bready Or Knot

Bready Or Knot is the second big game project I worked on. I made it with the same team as Apollyon. I did all the audio for the game. I had a great time and I believe it had some of my best work in it.















Harpoon Bird Fishing

Harpoon Bird Fishing is a game about shooting birds out of the air with harpoons as a sport. I did all the music as well as some playtesting.
